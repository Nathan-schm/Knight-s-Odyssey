# ⚔️ Knight's Odyssey
Knight's Odyssey is a 2D platformer where you play as a brave knight traveling across dangerous worlds — from icy tundras and scorching deserts to blazing volcanoes and the familiar lands of the normal realm.
Navigate treacherous platforms filled with deadly spikes, and fight off slimes that stand between you and the next world. Each realm brings its own unique challenges and atmosphere.

# 🌍 Realms

🌿 Normal World — your journey begins here
❄️ Ice World — slippery ground and frozen dangers
🔥 Fire World — lava, heat, and relentless enemies
🏜️ Desert World — scorching sands and hidden traps

# ⚙️ Features

Classic 2D platformer gameplay
Multiple unique worlds with distinct environments
Enemies: slimes and environmental hazards (spikes)
A brave knight as your hero
Boosts jumps
